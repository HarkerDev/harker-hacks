Harker Hacks was founded in 2017 by Harker students Arnav Joshi, Nakul Bajaj, Rohan Sonecha, and Nikhil Sharma, who serve as the lead coordinators for the event. Harker Hacks is an annual hackathon held over one weekend (24 hours) where attendees partner with other high schoolers to find innovative solutions to real world problems. Throughout the hackathon, we will have numerous workshops led by industry experts, where students can learn business and design skills. We are committed to providing an environment where everyone, regardless of experience or technical skills, can use their talents to innovate, and have mentors who can assist any competitors having difficulties.

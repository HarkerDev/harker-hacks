HarkerHacks was founded in 2017 as an annual hackathon held over one weekend (24 hours) where attendees partner with other high schoolers to find innovative solutions to real world problems.

Throughout the hackathon, we are committed to have a few things available to all participants:

- numerous workshops led by industry experts, where students can learn business and design skills
- an environment where everyone, regardless of experience or technical skills, can use their talents to innovate
- mentors who can assist any competitors having difficulties or who just want to learn more

The hackathon was founded by and is run by Harker students Nakul Bajaj, Arnav Joshi, Arjun Akkiraju, Rohan Sonecha, and Nikhil Sharma, who serve as the lead directors of the event.
